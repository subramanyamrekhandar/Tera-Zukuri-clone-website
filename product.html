The MM Based Dark-Brown with Doubled White




The 100% pashmina of the project MM are made from the diamond grade raw material harvest from the neck, chest and beard of the selected pashmina goat. The process are all made by hand of the skilful artisan of Mir family. With spacial technique, we add an extra layer of pashmina to middle section to make them extra warm. We hope our customer would be able to carry our product everywhere, therefore all Tera Pashmina will come with Tera hemp bag.
All the products are natural and hand made, there will be variation between each individual piece.

Weave Pattern : Classic Vertical
Edge Finishing : Hand Spin, Mid Length
Dimension : 175 cm * 85 cm (5 cm variants
Base Color : Dark-Brown
Length : 110 cm (5 cm variants)
Double Mid Color : White
Weight: 127.5 g
